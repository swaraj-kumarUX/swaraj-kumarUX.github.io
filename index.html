My path didn't begin in design — it began in conversations. Supporting customers at Amazon, I learned that every frustration is a design problem in disguise. Empathy became my first tool, and it still leads every decision I make.

Good design removes friction so quietly you never notice it. I obsess over flows, mental models and the small moments in between — turning research into interfaces that feel obvious, honest and human.
